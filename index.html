Discover the Books

Each book in the Percy Jackson series takes you on a thrilling adventure. From navigating the labyrinth to saving Olympus, each novel is packed with excitement, danger, and mythology!

Book List

  • The Battle of the Labyrinth: Percy Jackson faces the challenges of navigating the deadly labyrinth and defeating the monsters that threaten the world.
  • The Last Olympian: The final battle between the gods and the Titans unfolds in this thrilling conclusion to the series.
  • The Lightning Thief: Percy discovers he is the son of Poseidon and embarks on a quest to prevent a war among the gods.
  • The Sea of Monsters: Percy and his friends journey through the Sea of Monsters to retrieve the Golden Fleece that will save their home.
  • The Titan's Curse: Percy and his friends attempt to rescue Artemis and prevent a terrible prophecy from coming true.
